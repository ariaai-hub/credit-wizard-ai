§2 — How We Use Your Information

  • Open and manage your credit repair account
  • Prepare and file automated dispute letters on your behalf
  • Track and update you on the status of your disputes as bureau responses come in

  • Respond to your questions and support requests

FCRA compliance note
Credit Wizard AI is a software platform that assists with credit repair workflow. We are not a "consumer reporting agency" as defined under the FCRA. The platform facilitates your dispute filings but does not itself provide consumer reports.

§4 — Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted to and from our platform uses TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive data stored in our database is encrypted at rest
  • Access controls: Your account data is only accessible to you and the company that onboarded you
  • Audit logs: We maintain logs of account access and key actions for security review
  • Secure infrastructure: Our platform runs on Vercel and Cloudflare, with database hosting on Neon (PostgreSQL) — all with SOC 2 Type II compliant data centers
Important
While we maintain strong security practices, no system is 100% immune from breach. We commit to notifying you within 72 hours if we become aware of a data breach that affects your personal information.

§6 — Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can exercise these rights at any time by contacting us:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any inaccurate information in your file
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements for dispute records)
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities, such as automated decision-making related to dispute prioritization
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time
CCPA / CPRA rights
If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what data we collect, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of the "sale" of personal information (which we do not do). To exercise any right, email us at the address below.
